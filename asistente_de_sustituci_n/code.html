Don't have Shallots?

Shallots offer a delicate, sweet flavor with a hint of garlic. Here's how to pivot.

Red Onion

Ratio: 1:1 replacement

check_circle In your pantry

Chives

Ratio: 2:1 replacement

check_circle In your pantry

Best for raw applications or as a garnish. Adds that signature mild onion-garlic note without the crunch.
