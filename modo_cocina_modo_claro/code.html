Instructions

Add the diced chicken to the pan. Sauté over medium-high heat until the pieces are golden brown on all sides and cooked through.

tips_and_updates

Chef's Note

Don't crowd the pan! If necessary, cook the chicken in two batches to ensure it browns perfectly instead of steaming.
